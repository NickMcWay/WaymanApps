Privacy Policy

Momentum for iOS  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Momentum collects, how it is used, and what rights you have. The short version: Momentum stores all your personal data on your device only and does not collect or sell any personal information.

1. Who we are

Momentum is developed and published by Niels Weggeman, an independent iOS developer. You can reach us at hello@nielsweggeman.com.

2. Data stored on your device

All data you create in Momentum — tasks, projects, wins, streaks, badges, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device to our servers.
  • Is not shared with any third party by us.
  • May be included in iCloud backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device (this is controlled entirely by Apple and your iOS settings).

3. Subscription & payments

Momentum offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions (Monthly and Yearly) through Apple's In-App Purchase system (StoreKit). All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. We do not receive, store, or process any payment card information or billing details. For questions about billing, please contact Apple Support or manage your subscription in your App Store account settings.

4. AI Project Planner

Premium subscribers can use the AI Project Planner feature, which sends the goal text you type to a third-party AI API to generate task suggestions. By using this feature:

  • The goal text you enter is transmitted over HTTPS to the AI service provider.
  • We do not store your goal text on any server.
  • Do not include sensitive personal information (passwords, financial data, health records) in your goal descriptions.

5. Notifications

If you grant Momentum permission to send notifications, all notification scheduling happens locally on your device using iOS's UserNotifications framework. No notification data is transmitted to external servers.

6. Analytics & crash reporting

Momentum does not integrate any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Crashlytics, Mixpanel). We do not collect usage statistics, session data, or behavioural analytics.

Apple may collect aggregated, anonymised App Store analytics data (such as download counts and crash reports) in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no control over this data collection.

7. Advertising

Momentum does not display advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs or tracking identifiers (such as the Advertising Identifier / IDFA).

8. Children

Momentum is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.

9. Data deletion

You can delete all your Momentum data at any time by deleting the app from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored data. If iCloud Backup is enabled, you can also manage backups through your device's iCloud settings.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will provide notice within the app.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
